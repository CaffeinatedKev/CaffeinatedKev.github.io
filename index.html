Aeropress - Iced Coffee Recipe

275ml~

  • 1:10 coffee to liquid ratio
  • 17g Coffee
  • 170g Water
  • 60g Ice
Brew the coffee directly over ice and top up with milk, to taste.

500ml~

  • 1:8 coffee to liquid ratio
  • 30g Coffee
  • 240g Water
  • 80g Ice
Brew the coffee directly over ice and top up with milk, to taste.
